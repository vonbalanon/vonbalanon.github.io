Introduction

On & On

On & On is a community-centered independent print and boutique store in Downtown San Luis Obispo. We offer a selection of books, zines, posters, stickers, apparel and community events. A portion of our profits go to The Huey P. Newton Foundation — which maintains and assists Dr. Newton’s legacy and vision for the Black Panther Party.

Target audience

The target audience would be Gen-Z or Millenials that love reading informational books, zines, magazine of articles about politics and the society we live in. The audience are young teenager and adults that are eager to learn more from activists and writers like Angela Davis, James Baldwin and Langston Hughes. They also like random boutique items like apparel, stickers, and posters created by local artists.

On & On aims to help distribute educational materials, establish community events and uplift BIPOC voices in the San Luis Obispo community through the purchasing of their print and boutique items. Profits made go directly to employees as a liveable wage and a portion goes to The Huey P. Newton Foundation.

We Are

On & On helps distribute educational materials, establish community events and uplift BIPOC voices in the San Luis Obisp community. Our profits goes directly to pay our employees a liveable wage and a portion goes to The Huey P. Newton Foundation.

The Huey P. Newton Foundation:

The Huey P. Newton Foundation maintains and assists Dr. Newton’s legacy and vision for the Black Panther Part through the distribution of educational materials, establishment of educational conferences and forums, and preservation and exhibition of historical archives.

Our Values:

  • BIPOC groups deserve fair and accurate representation.
  • Young people's voices should be represented.
  • We deserve a voice in decisions that affect us.
  • We hold events that cover topics of marginalzied groups, elections and politics.
