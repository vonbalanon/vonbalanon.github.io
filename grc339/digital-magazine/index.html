Top Five Risograph Artists To Follow On Instagram

Risograph prints one vibrant color at a time, and it’s perfect for creating anything from posters, graphic prints, zines, postcards and more. Here are five Risograph artists on Instagram that prove that color is in and print is most certainly not dead.

Walk The Talk · A Multi-Site Exhibition For Cop26

Walk The Talk is a free multi-site exhibition set across the West End and Southside of Glasgow during COP26 from 1 to 12 November 2021. Curated by Concrete Nature, the exhibition features Risograph prints from UK creatives around the themes of nature and climate change.

Windrush Echoes Exhibitiony

The exhibition is a visual exploration into experiences of the Windrush generation, highlighting the harsh realities of life as an immigrant of Caribbean descent in the UK. It focuses on the experiences as an immigrant and the mistreatment of the Windrush generation, which included wrongful deportation to the Caribbean.

Meet Heather Chambers

Based in Stockport, a large town in Northern England, Heather Chambers is an illustrator and muralist. Her hometown that is one of the biggest influences on Heather’s creative output.

Meet Ollie Cooper

Lettering artist and illustrator Ollie Cooper’s work is bold and playful, providing commentary on subjects such as language, social, and environmental issues. Combining his branding background with his love of typography, his signature style fuses together language with bespoke lettering to create tongue-in-cheek, impactful pieces.

Michael Van Kekem: Riso Zine

Rotterdam-based creative Michael van Kekem’s practice covers a diverse range of fields including Illustration, graphic design, and printmaking. One of his latest projects is a Risograph printed zine.
