An Intro to Risograph Printing

Risographs took hold of the international art and design community almost four decades ago. Due to its affordability and small footprint, Riso became an easy way for artists and designers to produce their own work and publish work for others. Our friends at Risolve—a family-operated printing studio—join us today to shed light on the art of Risograph printing. Learn where it came from, what makes it unique, and how to start your very first Riso project.

Eight Risograph Artists To Follow on IG

Risograph printing is perfect for creating anything from posters, graphic prints, zines, postcards, and, well, you get the point. Here are eight Risograph artists on Instagram that prove that color is in and print is most certainly not dead.

Walk The Talk: An Exhibition For Cop26

Walk The Talk is a free multi-site exhibition in Glasgow during COP26 from November 1–12, 2021. Curated by Concrete Nature, the exhibition features Risograph prints from UK creatives around the themes of nature and climate change.

Lost Bratislava: Riso Book By Cierne Diery

Lost Bratislava is a Risograph-printed and Japanese hand-bound book made of 30 illustrations, and 1 custom font system, produced in and edition of 850 copies. All in all, the publication took about a year to create.

Rotterdam Art Prints: RAP Christmas cards

Meet Rotterdam Art Prints, aka RAP! A new printmakers collective based in the buzzing city of Rotterdam, The Netherlands. They promote local artists and host networking events to share their love of design and printmaking.

Dept. Store: 10 Colourful Prints

Happy 2022! It's a new year, that means new wall decor! We’ve selected 10 awesome and colourful Risographic prints perfect for brightening up your home, office, or studio space in 2022, refreshing your walls and bringing all the joy.

Every Thought You Ever Had Music Video

Julia Schimautz, a graphic designer and printmaker based in Cape Town, recently created a fully Riso-printed video for one of musician, Pan Well‘s, latest tracks. The song is about one’s thoughts, and emotions that come rushing all at once.

Windrush Echoes Exhibition

The Windrush Echoes Exhibition is a visual exploration into experiences of the Windrush generation. This exhbit of Risograph prints highlights the harsh realities and mistreatment of life as an immigrant of Caribbean descent in the United Kingdom.

Meet Heather Chambers

Based in Stockport, a large town in Northern England, Heather Chambers is an illustrator and muralist. Her hometown is one of the biggest influences on Heather’s creativity.

Meet Ollie Cooper

Lettering artist and illustrator Ollie Cooper’s work covers language, social, and environmental issues. Combining his branding background with his love of typography, his signature style fuses together language with bespoke lettering to create tongue-in-cheek, impactful pieces.

What's a Riso?

Risograph printing is a technique best described as a ‘digital screen printing’. The process is similar to screen printing, but with the convenience of an office copier. Learn more about Risography here!
