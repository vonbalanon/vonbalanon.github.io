Introduction

Ris Magazine

Ris Magazine is dedicated to highlighting everything happening in the world of risograph printing. We cover a variety of riso-related topics like getting started with riso prints, community events led by aspiring riso creatives or rising risograph artists you should follow. Join us and visualize the power of soy ink!
